The brief

A+ Mobile Detailing is a one-man LLC in St. Louis. Damon comes to you — no drop-off, no waiting. The site needed to turn more Google searches and referrals into calls.

The existing online presence was minimal. No website, just a name and a number. The work was good. The digital footprint wasn't keeping up.

Built for mobile service businesses where one call can turn into a repeat customer.

What we built

A light-mode one-page site built around A+'s existing brand — the mascot character, the blue and gold palette, the energy of a business that takes pride in the work. The hero puts the mascot front and center alongside a bold headline and a call button. Everything below supports the decision to book.

🎨
mascot-driven hero

the existing brand mascot anchors the hero section at full scale. the site feels like the brand, not a generic detailing template.

📦
tiered service packages

three clearly laid out packages with pricing and what's included. customers self-select before they call — shorter calls, better fits.

🏆
ceramic coating section

dedicated section for the premium service. breaks it out from the standard packages so it gets the attention a high-ticket upsell deserves.

📸
before & after gallery

real results, not stock photos. the gallery section lets the work do the selling — which is the most honest close there is.

📍
service area section

a dedicated section listing coverage areas across the st. louis metro. helps with local search and sets clear expectations.

📱
mobile-first, call-forward

the call button is the first thing a mobile visitor sees. sticky nav keeps it always visible. most customers land from their phone — the layout is built for that.

What makes it different

Most detailing websites look like they came from the same $12/month builder. This one was designed around what makes A+ actually different — the mascot, the LLC credibility badge, the "i come to you" positioning that removes the biggest objection before the visitor even asks.

The facebook link in the nav is intentional. It connects the existing social presence to the new web presence instead of replacing it — customers who already know Damon can find him where they expect, and new customers can see the history of real work.

The result

A professional site that looks and feels like a business that takes its work seriously. The call button is always visible. The packages are clear. The work speaks for itself in the gallery. A customer who lands on this page knows within five seconds what they're getting and how to get it.

Now when people search, click, and land here, they see packages, pricing, and a phone number in one place — fewer back-and-forth texts just to explain the basics.

This same pattern works for other local service businesses that live on calls.
